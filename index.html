About Me

With an extensive history in technology and the law, I have a unique background well suited for a growing software company. On the technology side, my past studies and engineering experience were concentrated in logic design, microcontrollers, microprocessors, and software progamming. Continuing in that vein, I'm now diving headfirst into full stack web development technologies that most software development companies use today.

On the legal side, I've worked both sides of the table on software and technology deals. Working with software and service providers, I prioritize understanding the underlying technology and business goals when crafting and negotiating license and related service agreements. My experience in representing licensees provides insight that is invaluable in getting the deal done. Beyond transactions, my experience with intellectual property issues often encountered by software companies enables me to help clients navigate those waters.
